ARA, GUR & SCY.

We may remark, though nothing can Dismay us,
That if you thwart this gentleman, He'll slay us.
We don't fear death, of course – we're taught To shame it;
But still upon the whole we thought We'd name it.

SCY.

Yes!

GUR.

Yes!

ARA.

Yes!

ALL.

Better perhaps to name it.

(Gama's sons - 1954 Production)

PRI.

Be reassured, nor fear his anger blind,
His menaces are idle as the wind.
He dares not kill you – vengeance lurks behind!
